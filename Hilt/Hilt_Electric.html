Sample of Projects Completed

Dollar General

New Brighton, Pa
  1. This was a new construction project. The Electrical contract included: electrical service, parking lot lights, interior lights, branch circuit wiring, network wiring, telephone and device installation.

Wendy's

Chippewa Twp., Pa
  1. This was a new construction project. The Electrical contract included: underground conduit to transformer pad, main electrical service, parking lot lighting, signage, drive-thru loop detector wiring, facade lighting, interior lighting, branch circuit wiring, network cable, telephone, equipment wiring and device installation.

Handel's Ice Cream

Chippewa Twp., Pa
  1. This was a new construction project. The electrical contract included: electrical service, parking lot lights, canopy lights, interior lights, branch circuit wiring, equipment wiring, network wiring, telephone and device installation.

Eat-n-Park Cookie Factory

Robinson Twp., Pa
  1. This was a build out inside of a distribution warehouse. The Electrical portion of this project involved rewiring a section of the warehouse and the cookie factory space was an electrical design build project. Load calculation, service equipment sizing, lighting design, electrical drawings, material pricing, equipment rentals and wiring installation was provided by Hilt Electric.

Eat-n-Park Supervisor Training Center

Homestead, Pa
  1. This project included both aspects of a design-build project and the standard repurposing of office space within the corporate headquarters building. The training center used the latest in programmable lighting controllers, as well as, remote blinds and retractable video screens. All service panel equipment, branch circuit wiring, and mux-link connections were made by Hilt Electric.

C & E Plastics, Inc.

Georgetown, Pa
  1. This was a design build project for a plastic tank manufacturing company. The building was 18,000 sq. ft. with an overhead crane. Hilt Electric performed all load calculations and sized the transformers, service panels, and feeders in accordance with calculations. A lighting contactor panel was built on site for the high bay and exterior lighting. An emergency lighting time delay feature was also installed to allow the emergency lights to remain on, until the high-bay lights come back on after a power outage. From start to finish, Hilt Electric installed all electrical components and equipment in this new manufacturing facility.

Centennial Capital, LLP.

Midland, Pa
  1. This was a design build project for an industrial property developer. The project contained multiple buildings and multiple phases to complete. Hilt Electric provided a new 400 amp three phase service to a 25,000 sq. ft. warehouse. Removed large quantities of copper for the customer and replaced it with aluminum, properly sized for the decreased load. Another phase of the project was the removal of old high voltage cables from a series of manhole ducts. A special rig was developed to aid in the recovery of the underground lead and copper materials.
